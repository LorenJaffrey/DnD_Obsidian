### Level 3
**Controlled Burn**
You unlock Fire spells. After upcasting a spell or critting in beastform, you may inflict Smoldering on a creature within Range 6.
### Level 7
**Flash Fire**
Inflict Smoldering when you crit with a Lightning spell.

**Fan the Flames**
Your Wind spells have advantage against Smoldering creatures.
### Level 11
**Wildfire**
(1/encounter) Deal LVL+WIL fire damage to all enemies with Smoldering within Range 6, ending the condition.
### Level 15
**From the Ashes**
(1/Safe Rest) Action (while Dying): Expend all remaining Hit Dice. You heal for the amount rolled, and deal that much fire damage to all adjacent enemies.
