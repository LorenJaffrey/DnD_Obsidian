### Level 3
**Twinned Threads**
Action: Link two creatures within Range 6 with an ethereal web. Linked creatures are Slowed. When you deal damage to a Linked creature, other Linked creatures take KEY damage (ignoring armor). You can maintain one thread.

**Spider Climb**
Gain the ability to climb featureless walls and ceilings. You may attack while climbing, as long as you have at least one hand touching a surface.
### Level 7
**Threadshot**
(1/encounter) Action: Restrain all Linked creatures (escape DC 10+KEY).

**Woven Threads**
Twinned Threads now targets up to three creatures.
### Level 11
**Arachnophobia**
(1/encounter) When you crit a Linked creature, other Linked creatures become Frightened of you on their next turn.
### Level 15
**Aspect of the Spider Queen**
You embody the traits of the Spider Queen’s brood. You have advantage against Slowed enemies, and ignore Difficult Terrain. You may change the damage type of your melee attacks to acid at will.
