### Level 3
**Prey for the Pack**
(1/encounter) Designate your quarry as Prey. When you hit your Prey in melee, your allies gain advantage on attacks against it until the start of your next turn.

**Lead the Hunt**
While leading your party, both you and your allies roll Stealth with advantage.
### Level 7
**The Lion's Share**
When your Prey dies, gain WIL+LVL Temp HP.
### Level 11
**Scent of Blood**
Gain both benefits of Hunter's Mark against Bloodied Prey.
### Level 15
**Ferocious Takedown**
The hunt never ends. If you kill your Prey with Final Takedown, gain another use of Prey for the Pack.
