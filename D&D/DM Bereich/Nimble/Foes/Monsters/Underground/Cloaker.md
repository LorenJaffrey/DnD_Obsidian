**Level:** 13 | **Size:** Large | **Group:** Underground
**HP:** x | **Armor:** x | **Speed:** Fly 10

**Passive**
* **Ambusher.** Cloakers always start first and heroes roll Initiative with disadvantage.
* **Mutual Harm.** You take half damage from attacks while grappling a creature (they take the other half).

**Actions**
* **Wrap.** 2d10+20. On Hit: Grappled (escape DC 16). OR:
* **Horrifying Wail.** DC 16 WIL save, or creatures within 6 spaces are Frightened and must spend 1 Action moving as far away as possible.
