**Level:** 16 | **Size:** Huge | **Group:** Underground
**HP:** 140 | **Armor:** Heavy | **Speed:** Burrow 8

**Passive**
* **Tremor Sight.** Advantage against creatures that moved since the worm’s last turn.

**Actions**
* **Crush.** Creatures in a 2×6 area take 50 damage on a failed DC 18 DEX save. (Creatures who fail can spend 1 Action to dive out of the way instead of taking this damage. They move half their speed and land Prone.) OR:
* **Bite/Swallow.** 1d4+40. On crit: Swallowed.

> **Swallowed.** You take 20 damage at the start of your turn. Your attacks cannot miss and ignore armor.
