**Level:** 10 | **Size:** Large | **Group:** Hill And Field
**HP:** 74 | **Armor:** Heavy | **Speed:** Burrow 6

**Passive**
* **Burrow Forth!** Combat with a Bulette starts with the heaviest character making a DC 14 DEX save or they are Grappled (escape DC 14) and take 1d12+20 damage (half on save).

**Actions**
* **Drag Below.** (A Grappled creature) 2d12 then drag below and burrow away. OR:
* **Leap & Bite.** (If not grappling) leap 6, and attack for1d12+20. On hit: Grappled.
