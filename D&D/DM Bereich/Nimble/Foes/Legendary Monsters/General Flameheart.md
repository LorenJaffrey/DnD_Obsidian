*Level 8 Solo Huge Flame Titan*

**HP:** 175 | **Armor:** Heavy | **Saves:** STR+ WIL+

> **Cinder Armor.** Immune to fire. When damaged, deal **5** fire damage to all adjacent creatures.

> **Extinguish Flame.** Cold or radiant crits extinguish all areas ignited by Molten Fury and suppress Cinder Armor for 1 turn.

---

**ACTIONS:** After each hero's turn, choose one:
* **Inferno Cleave.** Move 8 then strike a 2×2 square area for 2d10+10 fire damage.
* **Molten Fury.** Lob a molten fireball at the furthest hero not already in the flames, igniting a 2×2 square area. Creatures there take 2d10+10 damage; and another 10 damage at the end of each of their turns if they remain in the area.

---

**BLOODIED:** At **87 HP**, on his next turn, he uses Molten Fury a number of times equal to half the number of heroes (rounded up).
**LAST STAND:** Flameheart is dying! **80** more damage and he dies. Until then, the area of his attacks increase to a 3×3 square.
