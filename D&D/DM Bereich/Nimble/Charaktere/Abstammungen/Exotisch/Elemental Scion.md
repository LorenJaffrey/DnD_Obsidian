*An embodiment of one of the primordial energies. Through summoning or interplanar travel, you now find yourself in the mortal world, deprived of the currents in which you once swam.*

**Elemental Embodiment**
Choose an element to embody. You know cantrips from the associated spell school. (1/Safe Rest) Reaction: You become immune to your chosen element until the start of your next turn.
