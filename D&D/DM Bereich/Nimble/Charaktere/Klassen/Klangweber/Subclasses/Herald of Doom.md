### Level 3
**Hurt Me Twice**
(1/turn) Suffer a Wound. Repeat the next spell you cast for free.

**Repent**
You may recover an additional Wound when you Safe Rest. +2 max Wounds.
### Level 7
**Out of Time**
(1/Safe Rest) When you Field Rest, you may recover a Wound.

**The End is Nigh**
(1/turn) Suffer a Wound. Reduce the mana cost of your spell by the number of Wounds you have for the rest of your turn.
### Level 11
**Precipice of the Abyss**
While Dying, you cannot gain more than 1 Wound from a single instance, and ignore STR saves when casting spells.
### Level 15
**Sell the World**
(1/encounter) Action: Expend any number of Hit Dice. Heal or damage a creature within Range 12 equal to the amount rolled, then recover a Wound.
