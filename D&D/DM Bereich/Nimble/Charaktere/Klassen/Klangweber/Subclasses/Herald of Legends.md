### Level 3
**Recite Legend**
Reaction (when you attack or cast a spell): Expend a Songweaver’s Inspiration to invoke one of the following Legends on a target within Range 6.
* **Legend of Will.** Grant WIL×2 Temp HP.
* **Legend of Might.** Push WIL spaces and knock Prone.
* **Legend of Snark.** Deal WIL×2 psychic damage (ignoring armor).
* **Legend of Tragedy.** Inflict Slowed and Poisoned for one turn.
### Level 7
**Battle Ballad**
When you Recite Legend, you and all allies within 12 spaces who can hear you gain advantage on their next attack.
### Level 11
**Solo**
(1/Safe Rest) Action: Embody the spirit of a legendary hero. Gain LVL+WIL Temp HP. Your attacks deal +WIL psychic damage (ignoring armor). You may Recite Legend without spending Songweaver’s Inspiration. Solo ends if you have no Temp HP.
### Level 15
**Finale**
(1/encounter) 2 actions: Sacrifice all non-Temp HP. Deal LVL psychic damage (ignoring armor) to all enemies within 12 spaces who can hear you, then recite all four Legends.
