### Level 3
**Divine Potential**
Your Patron grants you knowledge of Radiant spells. Your Shadow Blast and minions can deal radiant or necrotic damage.

**Intervention**
Your shadow minions can Interpose for allies, but not yourself (1/round, does not cost you an action).
### Level 7
**See the Light**
(1/encounter) When a shadow minion Interposes for an ally, the attacker becomes Blinded until the end of its next turn.

**Serve the Light**
Choose 1 Radiant Utility Spell.
### Level 11
**Penance**
(KEY/Safe Rest) Until the end of your turn, damage dealt by your Shadow Blast and minions heal the target instead.
### Level 15
**Miraculous Revival**
(1/Safe Rest) Reaction (when an ally gains Dying): Heal your ally for half of their max HP and 1 Wound. All enemies within Reach 2 of that ally become Blinded until the end of their next turn.
