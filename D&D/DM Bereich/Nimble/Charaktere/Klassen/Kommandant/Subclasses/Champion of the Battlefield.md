### Level 3
**Strategic Instructions**
Action: Relay an Instruction to an ally that can hear you, granting them certain effects. You can have 1 Instruction active (new Instructions replace active ones). Instructions last until the end of combat or they are replaced. Known Instructions:
* **Get Back In Line!** +2 speed.
* **Slow Their Advance!** Attacks apply Slowed.
* **Get Their Attention!** Attacks Taunt the target until the end of their next turn.
### Level 7
**Stay in the Fight**
When relaying an Instruction to an ally, they gain KEY Temp HP. New Orders. You can now have up to 2 Instructions active. Learn a new Instruction:
* **Disrupt Their Formation!** Attacks Push 2 on hit.
### Level 11
**New Orders (2)**
You can now have up to 3 Instructions active. Learn a new Instruction:
* **Breach Their Defenses!** Adjacent allies gain advantage on attacks.
### Level 15
**Surprise Assault**
(1/encounter) Reaction: Grant all allies with Instructions an extra action.
