*Lightning Utility Cantrip*

**Casting Time:** 1 minute.
**Effect:** Conjure a Tiny (squirrel-sized) electrical helper for up to 1 hour. It can fetch Tiny objects (~1 lb./500 g max), open unlocked doors, illuminate a small area, or deliver a harmless shock. If it takes damage or moves further than 6 spaces away from you, it dissipates into sparks.
