*Tier 1 Wind Spell*

2 Actions | Single Target | Range 12

**Damage:** 3d4 bludgeoning, advantage against flying, Small, or Tiny targets. On hit: Move a Med target 2 spaces away; Small/Tiny twice as far; Large half as far (round down). For each die you would roll due to forced movement from this spell, deal +5 damage instead.
**Upcast:** +1 movement.
