*Tier 8 Ice Spell*

4 Actions | Single Target | Range 12

**Effect:** A target must make a STR save or become incapacitated in an icy tomb and immune to harm. This lasts as long as you remain alive, or until the ice melts or is otherwise destroyed. The creature may repeat the save once every 10 days. Huge or larger creatures have advantage on the save. A Small or Tiny creatures, disadvantage.
**Upcast:** +5 spell save DC.
