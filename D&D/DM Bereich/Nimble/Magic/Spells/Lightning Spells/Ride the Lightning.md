*Tier 6 Lightning Spell*

3 Actions | AoE

**Effect:** Teleport up to 12 spaces away to a spot you can see (if a willing creature is there, swap places with them). Adjacent creatures take d88 damage. Surviving creatures must make a STR save or be hurled back 3 spaces, knocked Prone, and deafened for 1 day.
**Upcast:** +1 DC.
