*Tier 3 Lightning Spell*

2 Actions | AoE | Line 12

**Damage:** 3d8+4 (ignoring metal armor). Surviving creatures are Dazed on a failed STR save, or Incapacitated instead for 1 of their turns if they fail by 5 or more. Creatures with a large amount of metal (e.g., armor or a longsword) roll with disadvantage.
**Upcast:** +4 damage.
