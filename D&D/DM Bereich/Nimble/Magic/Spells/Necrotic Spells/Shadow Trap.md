*Tier 1 Necrotic Spell*

2 Actions | Single Target

**Concentration:** Up to 1 minute.
**Effect:** The next creature to move adjacent to you suffers 3d12 damage; if Small or Tiny, it is also Restrained by shadowy tendrils for as long as you maintain concentration or until they escape.
**Upcast:** +1 size category, +1d12 damage when they escape.
