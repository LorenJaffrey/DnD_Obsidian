*Tier 7 Necrotic Spell*

3 Actions | AoE | Reach 8

**Damage:** 4d20. If this kills the creature, it violently erupts and you MUST deal the same amount of damage to another creature within 8 spaces of it that has not yet been damaged by this effect. Repeat until a creature survives this damage or no other creatures are within Reach.
**Upcast:** +1d20 damage.
