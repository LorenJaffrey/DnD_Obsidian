*(by Santuric)*

*Level 5 Large Solo Crustacean*

**HP:** 120 | **Armor:** Heavy | **Saves:** STR++ INT-

> **Carcinisation.** When Carcinicus damages a creature, it gains a stack of Crabby.

---

**ACTIONS:** After each hero's turn, choose one:
* **Scuttle.** Move 2, 4d4+6 damage.
* **Spit.** (Range 8) 2d4+4 damage.
* **Declaw.** (a creature with 2+ Crabby) Move 2, 1d4 damage. On hit: Lose all stacks of Crabby and suffer 1 Wound.

> **Crabby.** -1 Speed per stack. Action: Remove a stack of Crabby.

---

**BLOODIED:** At **60 HP**, Carcinicus summons 1 Lagoonionnaire per Hero. To me my legions!
**LAST STAND:** Carcinicus is falling! **50** more damage and he dies. Until then, he chooses twice each turn.
