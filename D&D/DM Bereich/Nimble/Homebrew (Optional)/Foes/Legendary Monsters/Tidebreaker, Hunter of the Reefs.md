*(by Santuric)*

*Level 7 Solo Large Coral Dragon*

**HP:** 180 | **Armor:** Heavy* | **Saves:** STR+ WIL+

> **Coral Armor.** Tidebreaker has Heavy Armor while the Coral Armor is intact. Gain a stack of Surge when damaged for 5 or more. Replace **X** with the amount of Surge stacks.

---

**ACTIONS:** After each hero's turn, choose one:
* **Ebb.** Fly 8, 1d10+**X** damage.
* **Flow.** (reach 2) 2d10+**X** damage. On hit: Push **X** spaces.
* **Undertow (1 use).** **X**d10 damage. On damage: Prone, Slowed and Dazed for 1 turn.

---

**BLOODIED:** At **90 HP**, Coral Armor breaks, suppressing the effect. Retain Surge stacks.
**LAST STAND:** Tidebreaker is dying! **70** more damage, and it's washed away. Until then it gains the following action: **Flash Flood.** Gain a stack of Surge.
